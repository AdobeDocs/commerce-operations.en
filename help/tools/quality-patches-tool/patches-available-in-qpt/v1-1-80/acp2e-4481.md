# ACP2E-4481: Bundle product saleability recalculated incorrectly after order cancel

The ACP2E-4481 patch fixes the issue where bundle product saleability is not recalculated correctly after an order is canceled. This patch is available when the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool (QPT)]](/help/tools/quality-patches-tool/quality-patches-tool-to-self-serve-quality-patches.md) 1.1.80 is installed. The patch ID is ACP2E-4481. Please note that this issue is scheduled to be fixed in Adobe Commerce 2.4.9.

## Affected products and versions

**The patch is created for Adobe Commerce version:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.7-p8

**Compatible with Adobe Commerce versions:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.7 - 2.4.8-p5

>[!NOTE]
>
>The patch might become applicable to other versions with new [!DNL Quality Patches Tool] releases. To check if the patch is compatible with your Adobe Commerce version, update the `magento/quality-patches` package to the latest version and check the compatibility on the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool]: Search for patches page](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/tools/commerce-quality-patches/index.html). Use the patch ID as a search keyword to locate the patch.

## Issue

Issue where bundle product saleability is not recalculated correctly after an order is canceled.

<u>Prerequisites</u>:

1. MSI is enabled.

<u>Steps to reproduce</u>:

1. Create a simple product. Set stock quantity to 6 and out-of-stock threshold to 5.
1. Verify that the calculated saleable quantity is 1.
1. Create a bundle product with a required option including the simple product with default quantity 1.
1. Verify both simple and bundle products are visible on Storefront.
1. Place an order for the bundle product.
1. Verify that both the simple and bundle products become out of stock.
1. Cancel the order in Admin without creating a shipment.

<u>Expected results</u>:

1. Both the simple product and the bundle product are returned to in-stock status after order cancellation.

<u>Actual results</u>:

1. Only the simple product is returned to in-stock status.
1. The bundle product remains out of stock and unavailable on Storefront.
