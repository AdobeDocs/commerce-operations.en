# ACP2E-4481: Bundle product saleability recalculated incorrectly after order cancel

The ACP2E-4481 patch fixes the issue where bundle product saleability is recalculated incorrectly after order cancellation. This patch is available when the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool (QPT)]](/help/tools/quality-patches-tool/quality-patches-tool-to-self-serve-quality-patches.md) 1.1.80 is installed. The patch ID is ACP2E-4481. Please note that this issue is scheduled to be fixed in Adobe Commerce 2.4.9.

## Affected products and versions

**The patch is created for Adobe Commerce version:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.7-p8

**Compatible with Adobe Commerce versions:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.7 - 2.4.8-p5

>[!NOTE]
>
>The patch might become applicable to other versions with new [!DNL Quality Patches Tool] releases. To check if the patch is compatible with your Adobe Commerce version, update the `magento/quality-patches` package to the latest version and check the compatibility on the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool]: Search for patches page](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/tools/commerce-quality-patches/index.html). Use the patch ID as a search keyword to locate the patch.

## Issue

Bundle product saleability is not recalculated correctly after an order is canceled.

<u>Prerequisites</u>:

MSI (Multi-Source Inventory) enabled.

<u>Steps to reproduce</u>:

1. Create a simple product with a stock quantity of `6` and an out-of-stock threshold of `5`.
1. Verify that the calculated saleable quantity is `1`.
1. Create a bundle product with a required option that includes the simple product with a default quantity of `1`.
1. Verify that both the simple and bundle products are visible on the [!UICONTROL Storefront].
1. Place an order for the bundle product.
1. Verify that both the simple and bundle products are out of stock.
1. Cancel the order from the [!UICONTROL Admin] without creating a shipment.

<u>Expected results</u>:

Both the simple product and the bundle product are restored to in-stock status after order cancellation.

<u>Actual results</u>:

1. Only the simple product is restored to in-stock status.
1. The bundle product remains out of stock and unavailable on the [!UICONTROL Storefront].
