# ACSD-67370: Incorrect prices were shown for Bundle products on PDP/PLP and the cart page for multi-currency stores

The ACSD-67370 patch fixes the multiple issues where incorrect prices were shown for Bundle products on product detail page (PDP)/product listing page (PLP) and the cart page for multi-currency stores. This patch is available when the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool (QPT)]](/help/tools/quality-patches-tool/quality-patches-tool-to-self-serve-quality-patches.md) 1.1.76 is installed. The patch ID is ACSD-67370. Please note that this issue is scheduled to be fixed in Adobe Commerce 2.4.9.

## Affected products and versions

**The patch is created for Adobe Commerce version:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.8-p3

**Compatible with Adobe Commerce versions:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.7 - 2.4.8-p3

>[!NOTE]
>
>The patch might become applicable to other versions with new [!DNL Quality Patches Tool] releases. To check if the patch is compatible with your Adobe Commerce version, update the `magento/quality-patches` package to the latest version and check the compatibility on the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool]: Search for patches page](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/tools/commerce-quality-patches/index.html). Use the patch ID as a search keyword to locate the patch.

## Issue

Incorrect prices are displayed for Bundle products on PDP/PLP and the shopping cart page for multi-currency stores.

<u>Steps to reproduce</u>:

1. Log into the Admin Panel.
1. Go to Catalog > Products > Add Product.
1. Create a simple product with SKU = "bundle-simple".
    1. Set price exactly $85.11
    1. Assign a quantity of at least 20 to the product.
    1. Assign a weight to the product.
    1. Assign product to the default category.
    1. Save the product.
1. Go to Catalog > Products > Add Product.
1. Select "Bundle Product" dropdown.
1. Create a bundle product with SKU = "bundle1".
    1. Assign to new category "cat1".
    1. Assign the simple product as an option of the bundle product.
        1. Scroll down to "Bundle Items" and press "Add Option".
        1. Name the option e.g., "opt1" and press "Add Products to Option".
        1. Select "bundle-simple" and press "Add Selected Products".
        1. Set the default quantity = 8.
    1. Save the bundle product.
1. Add an additional allowed currency.
    1. Go to Stores > Configuration > General > Currency Setup.
    1. Select both **USD** and **British Pound**.
    1. Save the configuration.
1. Set currency conversion rate for USD -> GBP.
    1. Go to Stores -> Currency -> Currency Rates.
    1. Enter **0.75** under GBP and press "Save Currency Rates".
1. Clear cache.
1. Reindex.
1. Open Storefront.
1. Add the bundle product to the cart.
1. Compare bundle product prices on Product Details Page, on the mini-cart, and in the shopping cart.

<u>Expected results</u>:

Product prices should be the same on Product Details Page, Product Listing Page (category page), in the Shopping Cart, and on the Mini-Cart.

<u>Actual results</u>:

Product price is different on Product Details Page / Product Listing Page, and in the Shopping Cart / Mini-Cart:
1. Product Details Page / Product Listing Page = 510.66
1. Shopping Cart / Mini-Cart = 510.64
