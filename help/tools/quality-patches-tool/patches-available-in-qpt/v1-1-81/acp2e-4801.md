# ACP2E-4801: Bundle product option quantities don't update when reconfiguring a negotiable quote in Admin

The ACP2E-4801 patch fixes the issue where bundle product option quantities were not updated when re-configuring a negotiable quote from the admin panel. This patch is available when the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool (QPT)]](/help/tools/quality-patches-tool/quality-patches-tool-to-self-serve-quality-patches.md) 1.1.81 is installed. The patch ID is ACP2E-4801. Please note that this issue is scheduled to be fixed in Adobe Commerce B2B 1.5.2-p4.

## Affected products and versions

**The patch is created for Adobe Commerce version:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) B2B 1.5.2-p4

**Compatible with Adobe Commerce versions:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) B2B >=1.4.2 <1.5.3

>[!NOTE]
>
>The patch might become applicable to other versions with new [!DNL Quality Patches Tool] releases. To check if the patch is compatible with your Adobe Commerce version, update the `magento/quality-patches` package to the latest version and check the compatibility on the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool]: Search for patches page](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/tools/commerce-quality-patches/index.html). Use the patch ID as a search keyword to locate the patch.

## Issue

Bundle product option quantities are not updated when re-configuring a negotiable quote from the admin panel.

<u>Prerequisites</u>:

Adobe Commerce [!DNL B2B] is installed.

<u>Steps to reproduce</u>:

1. In Admin, go to **[!UICONTROL Stores]** > **[!UICONTROL Settings]** > **[!UICONTROL Configuration]** > **[!UICONTROL General]** > [!DNL B2B] Features.
    1. Set **[!UICONTROL Enable Company]** to **[!UICONTROL Yes]**.
    1. Set **[!UICONTROL Enable B2B Quote]** to **[!UICONTROL Yes]**.
1. Create a bundle product with two options.
1. Check **[!UICONTROL User Defined]** check box for quantity.
1. Create a company account on the storefront.
1. Sign in with the company account and add the bundle product to the shopping cart.
1. Submit a negotiable quote request from the shopping cart.
1. In Admin, go to **[!UICONTROL Sales]** > **[!UICONTROL Quotes]** and open the created quote.
1. In the quote line item, click **[!UICONTROL Configure]**.
1. Change the bundle option quantity in the configuration dialog.
1. Click **[!UICONTROL OK]** and check the quantity shown in the quote.

<u>Expected results</u>:

The updated bundle option quantity should be reflected in the quote.

<u>Actual results</u>:

The updated bundle option quantity is not reflected in the quote.
