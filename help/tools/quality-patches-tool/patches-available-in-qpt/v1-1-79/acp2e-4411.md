# ACP2E-4411: Bundle product prices show incorrect tax and currency values between the Product Detail Page and cart in a multi-currency setup

The ACP2E-4411 patch fixes the issue where the incorrect price is shown for a bundle product on the cart page and in the mini-cart for multiple-currency stores. This patch is available when the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool (QPT)]](/help/tools/quality-patches-tool/quality-patches-tool-to-self-serve-quality-patches.md) 1.1.79 is installed. The patch ID is ACP2E-4411. Please note that this issue is scheduled to be fixed in Adobe Commerce 2.4.9.

## Affected products and versions

**The patch is created for Adobe Commerce version:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.8-p3

**Compatible with Adobe Commerce versions:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.8 - 2.4.8-p4

>[!NOTE]
>
>The patch might become applicable to other versions with new [!DNL Quality Patches Tool] releases. To check if the patch is compatible with your Adobe Commerce version, update the `magento/quality-patches` package to the latest version and check the compatibility on the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool]: Search for patches page](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/tools/commerce-quality-patches/index.html). Use the patch ID as a search keyword to locate the patch.

## Issue

Issue where the incorrect price is shown for a bundle product on the cart page and in the mini-cart for multiple-currency stores.

<u>Steps to reproduce</u>:

1. Set GBP as a secondary allowed currency.
1. Set the currency conversion rate for USD -> GBP to 0.74.
1. Configure 20% tax rate and create a tax rule.
1. Enable "Display Both Prices" (Including and Excluding Tax) on PDP and Cart:
1. Create a Simple Product with Price 40.25.
1. Create a Bundle Product with one option and assign the above simple product to the bundle as a required option with default quantity 8.
1. Open the bundle product's Product Detail Page (PDP) (GBP view) and observe the prices.
1. Add the bundle product to the cart, view the cart page and observe the prices.
1. Compare prices in shopping cart and on PDP.

<u>Expected results</u>:

Prices on PDP and in shopping cart should be the same.

<u>Actual results</u>:

Prices on PDP and in shopping cart are different.
