# ACP2E-4475: Order or filter by price not working for out-of-stock bundle products

The ACP2E-4475 patch fixes the issue where the products listing page incorrectly filters and sorts out-of-stock bundle products by price when the **[!UICONTROL Display Out of Stock Products]** option is enabled. This patch is available when the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool (QPT)]](/help/tools/quality-patches-tool/quality-patches-tool-to-self-serve-quality-patches.md) 1.1.79 is installed. The patch ID is ACP2E-4475. Please note that this issue is scheduled to be fixed in Adobe Commerce 2.4.9.

## Affected products and versions

**The patch is created for Adobe Commerce version:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.8-p3

**Compatible with Adobe Commerce versions:**

* Adobe Commerce (all deployment methods) 2.4.7 - 2.4.8-p4

>[!NOTE]
>
>The patch might become applicable to other versions with new [!DNL Quality Patches Tool] releases. To check if the patch is compatible with your Adobe Commerce version, update the `magento/quality-patches` package to the latest version and check the compatibility on the [[!DNL Quality Patches Tool]: Search for patches page](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/tools/commerce-quality-patches/index.html). Use the patch ID as a search keyword to locate the patch.

## Issue

The products listing page incorrectly filters and sorts out-of-stock bundle products by price when the **[!UICONTROL Display Out of Stock Products]** option is enabled.

<u>Steps to reproduce</u>:

1. On the Admin sidebar, go to **[!UICONTROL Stores]** > **[!UICONTROL Settings]** > **[!UICONTROL Configuration]**. In the left panel, expand **[!UICONTROL Catalog]** and select **[!UICONTROL Inventory]**.
1. Expand the **[!UICONTROL Stock Options]** section.
1. Set **[!UICONTROL Display Out of Stock Products]** to **[!UICONTROL Yes]**.
1. Click **[!UICONTROL Save Config]**.
1. Create a new category.
1. Create a few products and add them to the category.
1. Create two simple products:
    1. simple1: priced at $10 and set to **[!UICONTROL In Stock]** with a positive quantity.
    1. simple2: priced at $20 and set to **[!UICONTROL Out of Stock]**.
1. Create a bundle product containing both simple products as separate, required options.
1. Visit the category on the storefront and sort by **[!UICONTROL Price]**.

<u>Expected results</u>:

The bundle product is displayed with a price of $30 (combined price of $10 and $20) and is sorted accordingly among other products in ascending price order.

<u>Actual results</u>:

The bundle product displays $10 instead of $30 and is sorted incorrectly.
